Kenzo Dubois-Montrelay

Philosophy · Cognitive Science · Machine Learning

Student in Classes Préparatoires aux Grandes Écoles (MPI), working at the intersection of normative political philosophy, cognitive science, and machine learning. My current research explores formal criteria for political unacceptability and the structural genealogy of metacognition across biological and artificial systems.
